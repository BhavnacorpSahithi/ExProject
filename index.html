Amidst all the complexities involved in running an Online Fashion Store, retailers tend to forget that at the end of the day it’s about selling. They spend money on inventory, building a website, fulfillment, and marketing to bring people to their website, only to show them products with cookie-cutter descriptions. Gone are the days when a visitor would order from a website for convenience and lack of choice. Leveraging Fashion eCommerce Marketing services, thousands of new eCommerce websites are popping every day, hence customers today are rather spoiled for choice. If you don’t do a good job in describing your product, somebody else will. That’s why we get surprised when we see retailers who’re more worried about the color of the ‘Add to Cart’ button than the product description. The reason why product descriptions are so often ignored and not always up to the mark on online stores is that: Their inventory is too big They don’t have the marketing budget to write high quality product descriptions They decide to go with the default product description provided by the supplier They hire a copywriter who doesn’t have experience in writing for Fashion description The product descriptions are written to influence search engines, not the customers, etc
